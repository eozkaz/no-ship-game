Mode 1 — Items

Teams pick 5 items to survive on their island. Use items in sentences to avoid danger!

How to play
  1. Each team chooses 5 survival items (words / vocabulary) — without knowing their island yet!
  2. Teams are then assigned to different islands (tropical, desert, mountain…).
  3. Each round, every island gets a unique danger scenario.
  4. Teams discuss for 2 minutes how to survive — they must use their items in sentences!
  5. Teacher clicks an item badge when a team uses it → it gets crossed out.
  6. At the end of the round, teacher clicks "Roll Dice". Each team rolls the big dice one by one: 1–2 = –1 HP, 3–4 = same HP, 5–6 = +1 HP.
  7. 0 HP = rescued off the island. Last team surviving wins!
  8. End of game: each unused item = –1 HP. Use them all!
❓

Mode 2 — Questions

Upload a question CSV. All teams answer each round. Correct = roll the dice. Wrong = –1 HP!

How to play
  1. Teacher uploads a CSV with questions and answers.
  2. Teams are assigned to different islands.
  3. Each round: question shown, timer counts down, teams discuss.
  4. Teacher reveals the correct answer, then marks which teams got it right.
  5. Teams that got it WRONG automatically lose 1 HP.
  6. Teams that got it RIGHT roll the big dice: 1–2 = –1 HP, 3–4 = same HP, 5–6 = +1 HP.
  7. 0 HP = rescued off the island. Last team surviving wins!
